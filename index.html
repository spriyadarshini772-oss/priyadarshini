Featured Project

Superstore Sales Analysis

Analyzed 9,994 retail transactions to identify loss-driving discount patterns and uncover high-margin opportunities, enabling data-driven pricing and product strategy decisions.

Note: Dashboard interaction is demonstrated through analysis and insights due to Power BI sharing limitations.

Pipeline: Excel → SQL → Python → Power BI

Key Insights

  • Tables generated −$17.7K loss despite strong sales
  • Copiers delivered highest profit margins (~37%)
  • November consistently highest sales month
  • Central region has lowest profitability (~8%)

Business Recommendations

  • Reduce heavy discounting on loss-making products
  • Promote high-margin products like Copiers
  • Improve strategy in Central region
  • Leverage seasonal spikes for marketing

Key Learnings

  • Validated insights across Excel, SQL & Python
  • Understood impact of discounting on profitability
  • Improved storytelling with dashboards
