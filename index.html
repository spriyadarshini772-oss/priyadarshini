  • Furniture is the top revenue category but contains the most loss-making sub-categories. Tables and Bookcases both generate negative profit despite strong sales figures.
  • Discounting is the root cause — Tables offered at 40–60% discount generate an average loss of $55 per order, while the same product at under 10% discount is profitable.
  • November is consistently the peak month every year, with 2017 reaching $118K — nearly 50% higher than previous November peaks, indicating strong business growth.
  • Central region has the weakest profit margin at ~8% despite being the 3rd highest in total sales, suggesting a less profitable product mix or heavier discounting.
